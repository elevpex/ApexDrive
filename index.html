Common Questions

Everything you need to know

Can't find your answer? Chat with an agent →

Your rental includes the vehicle, basic liability insurance, 24/7 roadside assistance, unlimited mileage (on most vehicles), and free GPS. All mandatory taxes and airport surcharges are shown at checkout — before you pay. Optional extras like CDW insurance, child seats, and satellite radio are clearly listed as add-ons.
A security hold of $250–$500 is placed on your credit card at pickup (amount depends on vehicle class). This is a hold, not a charge — your card is not debited. The hold is released within 3–5 business days after return, provided there's no damage or extra charges. Debit cards are accepted on Economy class only.
We offer three tiers: Basic (third-party liability, included free), Standard CDW ($18/day — reduces your damage liability to $500), and Premium Full Cover ($29/day — zero liability, personal accident + effects included). Your personal auto insurance or credit card may already provide coverage — check before adding ours.
Economy, Compact, and Mid-size vehicles come with unlimited mileage. Luxury, Sport, and Exotic vehicles are subject to a 250 miles/day fair-use policy — additional miles are billed at $0.35/mile. This is shown clearly before checkout. If you're planning a long-distance trip in a premium vehicle, contact us and we can arrange an unlimited package.
Cancel any booking free of charge up to 24 hours before pickup. Cancellations within 24 hours of pickup incur a 1-day rental fee. No-shows are charged the full rental amount. Refunds are processed within 5–7 business days to your original payment method. Prepaid bookings may have different terms — always check your confirmation email.
Minimum age is 18 with a valid driver's license. Drivers aged 18–24 pay a young driver surcharge of $15–$25/day. Some vehicle categories (Luxury, Exotic, Sport) require a minimum age of 25. All drivers must have held a valid license for at least 12 months. International licenses are accepted with a valid passport.
